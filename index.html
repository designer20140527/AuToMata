Key Features

Buy and Hold for Instant Rewards

Start earning simply by holding $ATM. Every buy, sell, stake, and unstake transaction contributes to rewards distributed directly to holders as reflections. Liquidity is also automatically increased, strengthening the protocol and price stability.

Stake and Earn Passive Income

15% of the token supply is dedicated to stakers. Staking reduces the available supply, while rewarding you with an APY based on market conditions. Unstake anytime. Refer and Earn More

Our upcoming dashboard allows you to generate unique referral links for stakers. Earn a percentage of generated fees and staking rewards, distributed weekly.
